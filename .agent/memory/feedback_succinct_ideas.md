When generating or updating wiki idea pages, keep content succinct. Brief paragraphs, short bullet points, no verbose sections.

**Why:** User prefers idea briefs over detailed writeups. Aligns with the [[Over-Grooming Anti-Pattern]] — ideas should capture the requirement, not exhaustively document it.

**How to apply:** Idea pages should have a one-paragraph description, bullet-point grooming decisions, and minimal examples. No motivation essays, no multi-paragraph open questions, no dense acceptance criteria at the idea stage.
