# Hardcoded Investment Scale Thresholds

Unified mode Q5 ("What is the investment scale?") uses hardcoded dollar thresholds: Small (<$50K), Medium ($50K-$250K), Large (>$250K). These are organization-specific — $50K is "small" for an enterprise and "large" for a startup.

Unlike Q1 which has an "Other: [please specify]" option, Q5 has no escape hatch. Add an "Other" option and consider making thresholds configurable per project or org.
