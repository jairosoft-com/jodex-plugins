# Knowledge Management

Broader subject area covering how knowledge is captured, organized, maintained, and retrieved. The LLM Wiki pattern is a specific approach within this domain.

## Key Distinction: Compilation vs. Retrieval

Traditional approaches (RAG, ChatGPT file uploads, NotebookLM) retrieve raw chunks at query time and regenerate answers from scratch. The LLM Wiki approach compiles knowledge once into persistent, interlinked pages and keeps them current — the wiki is a compounding artifact.

## Application Domains

- **Personal** — goals, health, psychology, self-improvement
- **Research** — deep topic dives over weeks/months with evolving thesis
- **Reading** — companion wiki for books (characters, themes, plot threads)
- **Business/team** — internal wiki fed by Slack, meeting transcripts, project docs
- **Analysis** — competitive analysis, due diligence, trip planning, course notes

## Why LLMs Enable This

Humans abandon wikis because maintenance burden grows faster than value. LLMs don't get bored, don't forget cross-references, and can touch 15 files in one pass. Maintenance cost drops to near zero.
