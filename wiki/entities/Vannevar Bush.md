# Vannevar Bush

American engineer and science administrator who proposed the Memex in 1945 — a personal, curated knowledge store with associative trails between documents.

## Connection to LLM Wiki

The LLM Wiki pattern is related in spirit to Bush's Memex vision. Bush's concept was closer to a personal, actively curated knowledge base with valuable connections between documents than what the web became. The part Bush couldn't solve — who does the maintenance — is now handled by LLMs.
