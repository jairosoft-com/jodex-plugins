Primary developer and architect of [[Jodex Plugin Marketplace]] under **Jairosoft** (ramon@jairosoft.com).

Creator and maintainer of the five-plugin marketplace: [[QA Testing Plugin|jx-qa]], [[Knowledge Base Plugin|jx-kb]], [[Product Management Skills Plugin|jx-pm]], [[Developer Skills Plugin|jx-dev]], and [[Core Shared Conventions Plugin|jx-core]].
